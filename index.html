About

Kohde is made up of passionate people that like to solve problems, learn and build amazing solutions. We work closely with our clients in order to provide them with solutions that are tailored for their specific needs. We pride ourselves on delivering value to our clients by being innovative, honest and efficient.

Services

Our clients and their challanges are unique and therefore each solution is tailored to meet their exact needs. We therefore seperate our core services and include them if and as needed.

Analysis

Not sure how to tackle a specific business challange, or perhaps you would like to determine if there really is one. With plenty of knowledge on the technology, design and business side, we can lend a helping hand.

Prototyping

A prototype is worth a thousand meetings. Using a design thinking approach and rapid prototyping we are able to establish product functionality, flow as well as look and feel without writing a single line of code. This way you can determine whether your product is viable or not without running up costs.

Development

Our bread and butter. This is what we are good at. We work hard to stay up to date with the latest available technology and have covered everything from web development, cross-platform mobile development, system development and integration as well as K2 Smart Forms.

Support

We don’t just walk away after a product has been designed. Constant innovation is required to keep delivering meaningful and relevent solutions. With flexible support structures you are ensured that your product will remain competitive.
